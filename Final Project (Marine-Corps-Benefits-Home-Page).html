Marine Corps Values

Honor

Marines are led by their sense of honor to uphold the highest standards of morality and ethics. Never steal, never cheat, never lie; uphold an unwavering code of integrity; respect others' dignity. Marines are required by their code of honor to behave properly, carry out their duties, and hold themselves and others accountable for every action.

COURAGE

Marines are trained to have a strong mental, moral, and physical character. It helps us get through the difficulties of battle and helps us face our fears. The ability to uphold a higher standard of personal behavior, act honorably, and make difficult judgments under duress all come from our inner strength.

COMMITMENT

The tenacity and passion that characterizes Marines is known as commitment. The highest level of discipline for both individuals and groups results from it. It is the key component that permits ongoing commitment to the Corps and the nation. It motivates a steadfast drive to succeed in all endeavors.
